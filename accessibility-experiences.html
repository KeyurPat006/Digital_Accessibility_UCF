How to Play

Game Instructions

1️⃣

Read the Scenario

Each question presents a real-world development scenario and three different code or design options.

2️⃣

Choose Your Answer

Click or tap the option you believe best follows WCAG accessibility guidelines. You can use keyboard navigation too.

3️⃣

Learn & Advance

See instant feedback with a detailed explanation of why the correct answer is more accessible. Then move to the next question.
