FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this platform affiliated with UCF officially? +

This platform was created as an educational project by UCF students to help promote digital accessibility awareness. It is not an official UCF university service.

Can I use these resources in my own projects? +

Absolutely! All content on this site is educational and freely shareable. We encourage you to spread accessibility awareness. Please credit UCF Digital Accessibility when sharing.

How do I report an accessibility issue on this site? +

We practice what we preach! If you find an accessibility barrier on this site, please use the contact form above and describe the issue. We take accessibility feedback seriously and will address it promptly.

Does WCAG compliance guarantee legal protection? +

WCAG compliance significantly reduces legal risk and is referenced in most ADA lawsuits as the standard benchmark. However, it is not a legal safe harbor by itself. We recommend consulting an accessibility attorney for formal legal guidance.
